About

I am a Computer Vision Engineer with extensive experience in developing deep learning solutions for both medical and industrial imaging domains. My work focuses on designing robust segmentation, detection, and image enhancement models tailored to high-resolution and multi-modal data. I have contributed to real-world applications such as automated optical inspection (AOI) systems for electronics manufacturing and AI-driven diagnostic tools for radiology and oncology. Passionate about bridging cutting-edge research and practical deployment, I strive to build scalable, efficient, and interpretable AI systems that address complex visual challenges in high-impact fields.
